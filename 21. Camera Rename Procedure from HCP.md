# 1 Purpose

🎯 This document outlines the procedure for renaming a camera in **HikCentral Professional** and applying the updated camera name directly to the physical device.

The procedure ensures:

* ✅ Consistent camera naming
* ✅ Synchronization between software and device
* ✅ Improved monitoring and device identification
* ✅ Standardized surveillance management practices

### Step 1 — Open HikCentral Professional Web Login

# 3 Rename Camera in HikCentral Professional

## 🎥 Rename Procedure

### Step 1 — Navigate to Devices

Go to:

```text
Devices > Area
```

---

### Step 2 — Select Camera

Choose the camera name to rename.

### Example:

```text
office_Area
```

---

### Step 3 — Open Camera Configuration

Click on the camera.

---

### Step 4 — Rename Camera

There we can see the name of the camera.

Rename it.

### Example:

```text
Current Name : office_Area
New Name     : new_Office_Area
```

---

### Step 5 — Save Configuration

Click **Save**.

---

> ⚠️ Important Note:
>
> This rename is seen in **HikCentral Professional only**.
>
> We need to change it on the **device too**, not only in the software.

---

# 4 Apply Camera Name to Device

## 🔄 Device Synchronization Procedure

### Step 1 — Navigate to Devices

Go to:

```text
Devices > Area
```

---

### Step 2 — Select Camera

Choose the camera name to rename.

### Example:

```text
office_Area
```

---

### Step 3 — Tick the Camera Checkbox

Tick mark on the side of the camera.

---

### Step 4 — Apply Camera Name

Look for the option called:

```text
Apply Camera Name
```

📌 This option is available in the **top navigation menu bar**.

---

### Step 5 — Confirmation Popup

After clicking the option, a popup message will appear as:

```text
Apply: If camera name exceeded 32 characters, the name will not be fully displayed after being applied.
```

Click **OK**.

---

### Step 6 — Wait for Synchronization

Wait for the moment to apply.

---

### Step 7 — Successful Application Message

After successfully applied, it will show in green text as:

```text
Applying to all succeed
```

---

### Step 8 — Close Popup

Click **Close**.

---

# 5 Verification via Camera IP Login

## 🌐 Verification Procedure

Verify it by logging into the camera IP.

---

### Example Camera IP

```text
192.168.1.20
```

---

### Step 1 — Open Camera IP

Open the camera IP in the browser.

---

### Step 2 — Login with Credentials

```text
Username: admin
Password: *****
```

---

### Step 3 — Authenticate

Click **Login**.

---

### Step 4 — Verify Device Name

Go to **Live View**.

We can see the device name has been renamed from:

```text
office_Area
```

to:

```text
New_office_Area
```

---

### Step 5 — Close Browser

Close the browser after confirmation.
